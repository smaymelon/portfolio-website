Get to Know Me

Diversity

First born American

I am the first born American in a family where everyone else was born in South Africa. As a first born I have had the fortune of finding a medium of my south African culture and western culture. I bring the diversity of my country and that of many Americans who come from immigrant families. Coming from the melting pot country I have a deep blend of a multicultural background. Throughout my high school to college life, I also stood for LGBTQ+ and gender quality rights. I have spoken to faculty in my own college to educate them upon LGBTQ+ and gender quality rights for students as the President of PRIDE in Action! (LGBTQ+ club).
